# Privacy Policy — Game Advisor

**Effective date:** March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Game Advisor** (Android package: `com.siorre.gameadvisor`) handles information when you use the app.

## Summary
- We do **not** require an account to use the app.
- We do **not** sell your personal information.
- The app stores some data **locally on your device** (library/collections/reviews/search history/cache).
- The app uses a backend (Supabase) to fetch game data and news and to prevent abuse (rate limiting).

## Information We Collect / Process

### 1) Data stored locally on your device
Game Advisor stores the following on your device using local storage:
- Your **library**, **collections**, and **reviews/notes** you create in the app
- **Search history** (what you search for in the app)
- Cached content to improve performance and reduce network use (e.g., game lists/details, news)

This local data stays on your device unless you delete the app or clear app storage.

### 2) Data sent over the network
When the app fetches content, it sends requests to our backend and/or third-party services. These requests may include:
- The game/news **query terms** you searched for (to return results)
- A **device identifier** generated by the app and stored locally (used for abuse prevention/rate limiting)
- Your **IP address** (typically visible to any server you connect to)

### 3) Backend-stored data (limited)
To improve news syncing and reduce spam/abuse, our backend may store:
- **Aggregated search terms** (e.g., term + count and timestamps) used to decide what news to sync more often

**Please do not enter sensitive personal information into search fields.**

## How We Use Information
We use the information described above to:
- Provide app functionality (game details, screenshots/trailers, recommendations, news)
- Maintain caches and improve performance
- Prevent abuse/spam via rate limiting

## Third-Party Services
Game Advisor relies on:
- **Supabase** (backend hosting, database, edge functions)
- **RAWG** (game data; accessed via our backend proxy)
- **GNews** (news data; fetched by backend and cached)

These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

## Sharing
We share information only as needed to operate the app (e.g., sending requests to Supabase/RAWG/GNews). We do not sell your personal information.

## Data Retention
- **On-device data:** retained until you delete it (by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app).
- **Backend aggregated terms/logs:** retained as needed for operational purposes (spam prevention, sync optimization).

## Security
We take reasonable measures to protect the backend and limit access. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Your Choices
- You can clear app data by clearing storage/cache in Android settings or uninstalling the app.
- Avoid entering personal/sensitive information into search fields.

## Children’s Privacy
Game Advisor is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above will reflect the latest version.
